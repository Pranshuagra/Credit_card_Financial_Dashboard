# 💳 Credit Card Customer & Transaction Analysis — Power BI Dashboard

## 📌 Project Overview
Yeh project ek **end-to-end Credit Card Analytics Dashboard** hai jo Power BI mein banaya gaya hai. Dashboard do main reports mein divide hai:

1. **Credit Card Customer Report** — customer demographics, income, revenue aur interest earned ka analysis
2. **Credit Card Transaction Report** — transaction patterns, card usage methods aur expenditure behavior ka analysis

Iska goal hai bank/financial institution ke credit card portfolio ko understand karna — kaunse customer segments zyada revenue generate kar rahe hain, konse card types popular hain, aur transaction trends kaise change ho rahe hain quarter-over-quarter.

---

## 🎯 Objective
- Customer base ko demographics (age, gender, education, job, state) ke basis par segment karna
- Card category-wise (Silver, Blue, Gold, Platinum) revenue contribution identify karna
- Transaction behavior (Swipe/Chip/Online) aur expenditure patterns samajhna
- Quarterly revenue aur transaction count trends track karna
- Business ke liye actionable insights nikalna jo customer targeting aur product strategy mein help kare

## 📊 Dashboard Pages

### 1️⃣ Credit Card Customer Report
**Key KPIs:**
- Revenue: **55M**
- Customer Rating: **3.19**
- Interest Amount: **7.84M**
- Total Income: **576M**

**Visuals Included:**
- Customer Summary table (job-wise income, revenue, interest earned)
- Gender-wise revenue trend (Male vs Female, Jan–Oct 2023)
- Revenue by State Code (TX, NY, CA, FL, NJ)
- Revenue by Income Group (High/Med/Low)
- Revenue by Age Group (20–60+)
- Revenue by Education Level (Graduate, High School, Doctorate, etc.)
- Revenue by Job Type (Businessman, White-collar, Govt, etc.)
- Filters: Quarter (Q1–Q4), Week Start Date, Card Category, Income Group, Gender

**Key Insight:** Businessman category customers generate the highest revenue (17M), aur 40-50 age group sabse zyada revenue contribute karta hai (24M).

---

### 2️⃣ Credit Card Transaction Report
**Key KPIs:**
- Revenue: **55M**
- Transaction Count: **656K**
- Interest Amount: **7.84M**
- Total Transaction: **45M**

**Visuals Included:**
- Transaction Summary table (card category-wise revenue & interest)
- QTR Revenue vs Total Transaction Count (combo chart)
- Revenue by Card Using Method (Swipe, Chip, Online)
- Revenue by Card Type (Blue, Silver, Gold, Platinum)
- Revenue by Expenditure Type (Bills, Entertainment, Fuel, Grocery, Food, Travel)
- Revenue by Education & Job Type
- Filters: Quarter, Week Start Date, Card Category, Income Group, Gender

**Key Insight:** Blue card category dominates with **46M revenue**, aur **Swipe** transactions sabse zyada revenue generate karte hain (35M) as compared to Chip (17M) aur Online (3M).

---

## 🔑 Key Business Insights
- 💰 **Total Revenue:** ₹55M across all customer segments
- 👥 **Gender Split:** Male customers (30M) generate more revenue than Female (25M)
- 🏆 **Top Card Category:** Blue card contributes **83%+** of total revenue
- 📈 **Best Quarter:** Q3 leads with highest revenue (14.2M) and transaction count (166.6K)
- 🎓 **Education Impact:** Graduate customers contribute the highest revenue (22M)
- 🛍️ **Spending Pattern:** Bills (14M) aur Entertainment (10M) top expenditure categories hain
- 🌎 **Top States:** Texas, New York aur California equally lead in revenue (13M each)
