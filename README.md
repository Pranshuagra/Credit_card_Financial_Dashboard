💳 Credit Card Financial Analytics Dashboard

📖 Overview

The Credit Card Financial Analytics Dashboard is an end-to-end Business Intelligence solution developed in Microsoft Power BI to analyze customer behavior, transaction patterns, revenue generation, and financial performance.

The project transforms raw credit card transaction and customer data into meaningful insights through interactive visualizations, KPI tracking, and dynamic filtering capabilities. It enables stakeholders to identify profitable customer segments, understand spending habits, monitor transaction trends, and support strategic business decisions.

This solution consists of two integrated dashboards:

Credit Card Transaction Dashboard
Credit Card Customer Dashboard

Together, these dashboards provide a 360° view of credit card business performance and customer analytics.

🎯 Business Problem

Financial institutions generate millions of transactions daily, making it difficult to manually monitor customer activity, spending behavior, and revenue trends.

The objective of this project is to:

Analyze overall business revenue performance.
Monitor transaction volume and spending trends.
Identify high-value customer segments.
Understand customer demographics and financial behavior.
Evaluate card category performance.
Discover revenue-driving occupations, income groups, and regions.
Support data-driven decision-making using visual analytics.
